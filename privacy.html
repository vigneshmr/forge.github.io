Privacy Policy

Forge

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Forge is designed to be simple and private. This page explains what data the app does — and does not — collect.

Data collection

Forge does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on any server. Forge has no user accounts, no sign-in, and does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Where your data lives

All the activities, history, and streaks you create in Forge are stored locally on your device only, in a local database. Nothing you enter into Forge is sent over the network. If your device has iCloud or iTunes/Finder backups enabled, your Forge data is included in those backups like any other app data on your device — it stays under your control and is never accessible to us.

Third parties

Forge does not share any data with third parties, because it does not collect or transmit any data in the first place.

Children's privacy

Forge does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, since it does not collect information at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in the future (for example, if a new feature introduces any data collection), this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will reflect the change.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be raised via GitHub Issues.
