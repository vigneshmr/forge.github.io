Forge

Build habits that stick.

Forge is a simple, focused activity tracker built around one idea: consistency compounds. Set up the routines you want to build, check them off day by day, and watch your progress take shape — with streaks, and a clean Trends view to see it all at a glance.

Features

  • Track daily, recurring activities with custom schedules
  • Build and maintain streaks — only scheduled days count
  • Visualize progress in Day, Week, and Month trend views
  • Reorder and edit activities with a simple drag-and-drop list
  • No ads, no social feed, no clutter

Frequently asked questions

Is my data backed up?
Forge stores your data on your device and includes it in your regular iCloud/iTunes device backups.
Does Forge require an account or internet connection?
No — Forge works fully offline and doesn't require a sign-in.
How do streaks work?
A streak only counts days an activity is actually scheduled, so a planned day off never breaks your streak.
